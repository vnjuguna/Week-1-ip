About Me

Background

Violet is a motivated innovation champion, skilled solution architect and product manager. She manages the health portfolio within the Technology For Development Team in Safaricom PLC. Her current role entails forging meaningful partnerships with like minded organizations, with a goal of solving societal challenges, coming up with sustainable solutions while leveraging on technology to address these problems. Her most recent accomplishments include being part of the cross functional team that was instrumental in the delivery of the COVID-19 helpline for the country. Violet has always been passionate about innovating with customers, and in her previous role at Vodafone Global Enterprise, she successfully ran innovation workshops with Enterprise businesses to tailor make solutions in line with their strategic goals. She holds a Masters Degree in Business Administration with a focus on Strategic Management from the University of Nairobi, and Bachelor of Science in Information Technology from the Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology. She is currently studying Software Engineering from Moringa school due to her passion for technology.
